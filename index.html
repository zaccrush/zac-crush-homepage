Bridging Communities, Inspiring Fighters, and Elevating Martial Arts Across SE Asia

From Humble Beginnings to Regional Grappling Icon

I know what it’s like to walk around scared — unsure if you could protect yourself or trust anything else about who you were. For me, that all changed when I was fourteen, humiliated one last time in front of everyone during a playful wrestling match. Everyone was staring, and that moment lit a fire inside me. Since then, I’ve dedicated myself to martial arts, not just to build skill and confidence, but to create a community where no one feels powerless or alone. Moving to Southeast Asia, competing across the region, and launching events like Saigon Superfights, I’ve worked to uplift fighters and grow martial arts in places that need it most.
