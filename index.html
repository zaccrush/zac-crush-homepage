Bridging Communities, Inspiring Fighters, and Elevating Martial Arts Across SE Asia

From Humble Beginnings to Regional Grappling Ambassador

I know what it’s like to walk around scared — unsure if you could protect yourself or trust anything else about who you were. For me, that all changed when I was fourteen, humiliated one last time in front of everyone during a playful wrestling match. Everyone was staring, and that moment lit a fire inside me. Since that moment, I dedicated myself to martial arts to build not just skill, but real confidence. My mission became clear: to build a community where no one would ever have to feel that way.

When I moved to Southeast Asia, I saw a passionate fight scene that lacked professional opportunities. I knew I couldn't build something alone, so I partnered with the respected leaders of the Vietnam martial arts and grappling community. We quickly realized we shared the same vision: to elevate local athletes and give them the platform they deserved.

From that partnership, we created and executed events like Saigon Superfights. Together, we handled everything from matchmaking to running the event, always focused on the fighters. Our goal was to build a professional stage for athletes to showcase their skills and grow the sport.

Everything we do is about creating a strong community where people feel confident and supported, never powerless or alone.
