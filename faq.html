What is Gods of Martial Arts (GMA)? >

Gods of Martial Arts (GMA) is a premier Vietnamese combat sports organization and media property founded by the celebrated martial artist, actor, and cultural icon, Johnny Trí Nguyễn. Launched with the ambitious goal of elevating Vietnam's status in the global combat sports arena, GMA serves as a multifaceted platform for athletic competition, talent development, and cultural promotion. At its core, GMA is more than just a fight promotion; it is a comprehensive ecosystem designed to professionalize martial arts in Vietnam and establish the nation as a central hub for combat sports entertainment in Southeast Asia.

GMA celebrates ancient martial arts traditions, such as Kun Khmer (Cambodia), Muay Thai (Thailand), Lethwei (Myanmar), Vovinam (Vietnam), among others. GMA also strives to treat fighters as celebrities in its media-centric approach.

What is Saigon Superfights? >

Saigon Superfights is a Vietnam-based grappling event series founded by Zac Crush and organized by The Vietnam Grappling Podcast. It is a community-wide effort that involves the contributions and resources by several gyms throughout Saigon, including Arkain Grappling, RAM Muay Thai, Saigon Grappling Academy, Team Shark Saigon, Thao Dien Jiu Jitsu, Soi Jiu Jitsu, among others.

Saigon Superfights showcases top BJJ talent, particularly from the Saigon area, and aims to elevate grappling in Vietnam by providing a platform for athletes to compete and gain recognition. Saigon Superfights events are innovative and exciting, introducing live DJs, prize money, and sponsorships that provide opportunities for local grapplers to compete internationally. The events feature both superfights and tournament brackets, often with prize money and opportunities for international competition.

What is the SEA Grappling United App? >

The SEA Grappling United App was born from a discussion between Zac Crush and Khun Sa (Myanmar) on how to create more opportunities for Burmese fighters. Their collaboration led to the development of this mobile-friendly app, designed for anyone to track grappling events in under-resourced communities throughout Southeast Asia, including Myanmar.

The SEA Grappling United App is at the forefront of a collective effort to grow grappling initiatives in the region. It aims to centralize and consolidate efforts and resources, ultimately increasing the number of events and competitions available.

Designed with the user experience in mind, the mobile web app makes it easy for everyone to understand the who, what, and where of available resources. It is the first platform of its kind that seeks to build upon all community efforts in the region to provide more opportunities for everyone involved in the sport.

Which city in Vietnam has the biggest combat sports scene? >

The Epicenter - Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon): Ho Chi Minh City, still widely known as Saigon, is the undisputed heart of Vietnam's combat sports scene. It is home to the most mature and diverse ecosystem in the country, offering a staggering array of options for every type of practitioner. From sprawling, world-class fitness complexes and legendary, culturally significant martial arts centers to highly specialized, coach-centric dojos focusing on a single discipline, HCMC has it all. This is the epicenter, where the highest concentration of elite coaches, professional fighters, and dedicated hobbyists converge.

What martial arts gyms are in Saigon (HCMC)? >

Liên Phong MMA Centre

Description: More than just a gym, Liên Phong is a cultural institution and the spiritual home of modern Vietnamese MMA. Founded and run by martial arts icon Johnny Trí Nguyễn, it was the first MMA training center in the country. The center is dedicated to preserving and evolving the traditional Vietnamese martial art of Võ Liên Phong, founded by Nguyễn's grandfather, while adapting it for the modern MMA landscape. The facility itself is a remarkable 1800-square-meter campus designed in a traditional Southern Vietnamese style. It features an international-standard MMA cage and serves as the home base of the Gods of Martial Arts (GMA) promotion.

Owner/Founder: Johnny Trí Nguyễn.

Teachers/Coaches: Training is led by Johnny Trí Nguyễn himself, alongside a team of Vietnamese MMA champions.

Saigon Sports Club (SSC)

Description: If Liên Phong is the cultural soul of Vietnamese MMA, Saigon Sports Club is its modern, high-performance engine. Billing itself as Asia's largest mixed martial arts and cross-training center, SSC is a massive 7,500-square-meter, state-of-the-art facility in District 7. It is a premium, all-in-one complex offering an elite training experience across a comprehensive range of disciplines. The club's professionalism and world-class facilities have made it a destination for international athletes, famously hosting UFC star Dan Hooker for a full training camp.

Owners/Management: A key public figure at the club is Anthony Luu, the Front of House Manager and the organizer of the successful Saigon Open BJJ tournaments.

Teachers/Coaches: SSC prides itself on its roster of elite international and local coaches. Notable instructors and athletes associated with the gym include ONE Championship fighters Ev "E.T." Ting and Huynh Hoang Phi.

Are there gyms in Da Nang, Hoi An, or Nha Trang? >

Introduction to Coastal Training

Vietnam's stunning coastline has given rise to a unique martial arts sub-culture. The gyms in Da Nang, Hoi An, and Nha Trang have adapted to this market, offering everything from intensive fight camps to holistic retreats that blend combat sports with a beach-centric lifestyle.

Da Nang Deep Dive

Da Nang's scene is a compelling mix of large fitness centers incorporating combat sports, and a growing number of highly specialized, world-class martial arts academies.

  • BJJ Da Nang - Renzo Gracie Academy: The official Renzo Gracie affiliate in Da Nang, this academy is a cornerstone of the city's burgeoning grappling community. Led by the dedicated husband-and-wife team of Duy Thanh and his wife, the gym provides authentic, high-level Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu instruction in a welcoming environment for all levels. It represents a direct link to one of the most respected lineages in the sport.
  • MMA GYM ĐÀ NẴNG: This is one of the city's premier dedicated combat sports facilities, offering professional courses in MMA, Muay Thai, Boxing, and BJJ with modern equipment.
  • HD Fitness Center: A prominent fitness chain with multiple branches that have integrated MMA/boxing rings, signaling a clear move to cater to the growing demand for martial arts training.

Hoi An Deep Dive

The historic town of Hoi An has cultivated a martial arts scene built around the retreat model, tailored for travelers seeking a holistic experience.

  • Hoi An Muay Thai: A dedicated Muay Thai gym perfectly situated just two minutes from Cửa Đại Beach, offering an authentic training experience in an open-air setting with ocean views.
  • Naia Hoi An: A wellness and retreat organizer that provides multi-day packages combining daily Muay Thai sessions with yoga, meditation, and spa treatments.

Nha Trang Deep Dive

Nha Trang offers a surprisingly robust and well-credentialed fight scene that should not be overlooked by serious practitioners.

  • SILVERBACK MMA VIETNAM: A leading martial arts organization in Nha Trang, SILVERBACK offers a complete suite of combat sports, including MMA, BJJ, Muay Thai, and Boxing.
  • NT SPORT UNITE: Perhaps the most impressive facility on the coast in terms of coaching credentials. Their coaching roster is exceptional, including world cup champions and masters of sport, making it a hub for serious, high-level training.
